# OSEE Prep Hub — Launch Narrative & Press Kit

## Tagline
**"The magazine that teaches English."**

## The Story in 100 words
Indonesian teachers spend hours building syllabi, grading essays, and tracking student progress — all on platforms designed for tests, not for teaching. OSEE Prep Hub unifies the chaos: 4 practice platforms, EduBot, and the entire OSEE ecosystem, into one magazine-style workspace. AI tutors coach students 24/7, AI examiners grade with official IELTS rubrics, and OSEE Passport turns student achievements into cryptographically-verified credentials employers and universities trust. Result: teachers teach, students learn, and everyone has time for what matters.

## The Three Pillars

### 1. AI Teaching Assistant
- **Curator** — co-author a 4-week syllabus in 3 prompts
- **Tutor** — patient Socratic coach available 24/7 in Bahasa Indonesia
- **Examiner** — grades essays with official IELTS/TOEFL/TOEIC rubrics
- **Mentor** — long-term career planning in years, not weeks

### 2. OSEE Passport
Cryptographically-signed credentials that travel with the student:
- IELTS / TOEFL / TOEIC score reports
- Course completions
- Badges (100-day streak, perfect week, etc.)
- Teacher recommendations

Verifiable by anyone at `/verify/:credentialId` — no login, no app, no friction. Public key at `/.well-known/passport-public-key.pem`.

### 3. Magazine Workspace
Not another LMS. A real magazine-style editor where teachers build syllabi the way journalists write stories — with kickers, drop caps, gold rules, and the pleasure of typography. Students get a weekly "issue" instead of another notification.

## Why Now

**The numbers:**
- 270 million people in Indonesia
- 70 million students learning English
- 1.4 million English teachers
- $2.3B spent annually on English test prep
- Zero platforms connect the practice + test prep + career pipeline

**The problem:**
Teachers juggle 5+ apps (Edmodo, Google Classroom, WhatsApp groups, the test platform, the AI tutor). Students lose motivation between apps. Employers can't verify the certificates floating around.

**OSEE's bet:**
Make the workspace so good teachers want to use it, so credible employers trust the credentials, and so addictive students open it daily. Then the AI agents do the heavy lifting (grading, tutoring, scaffolding) so the humans focus on what humans do best.

## FAQ

**Q: How is OSEE different from Duolingo, Cambly, or other English apps?**
A: OSEE is a workspace FOR teachers, not a student app. The teacher is the customer. The AI agents work for the teacher. Students are downstream of the teacher's workspace.

**Q: Why Indonesia?**
A: 70M students, 1.4M teachers, $2.3B market, near-zero competition for "teacher's workspace". Plus, Bahasa fallback for AI is a hard technical problem that becomes a moat.

**Q: What's the open source story?**
A: OSEE will release the agent prompts and Passport ledger code as open source in 2025. We believe verifiable credentials should be a public good.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: B2B SaaS for schools + commission on marketplace + Passport verification fees for employers. B2C is freemium.

**Q: How big can this get?**
A: Southeast Asia has 650M people learning English. If we capture 10% of the addressable market at $5/user/month, that's $390M ARR. Plus employer Passport verification at $10/credential × 5M verifications/year = $50M.
