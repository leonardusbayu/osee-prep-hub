# CEFR B1 Writing Descriptors

## Overall Written Production

At B1 level, writers can:
- Write straightforward connected texts on familiar subjects of interest
- Link sentences into a coherent paragraph using basic connectors
- Describe experiences, events, dreams, hopes, and ambitions
- Briefly give reasons and explanations for opinions and plans
- Narrate a story or relate the plot of a book or film

## Writing Formats

### Informal Letters/Emails
B1 writers can:
- Write personal letters describing experiences, feelings, and events
- Use appropriate informal register (greetings, closings)
- Maintain a logical sequence of events

### Formal Letters
B1 writers can:
- Write simple formal letters asking for information or making a complaint
- Use basic formal expressions (Dear Sir/Madam, Yours faithfully)
- State the purpose of the letter clearly

### Essays and Reports
B1 writers can:
- Write short, simple essays on topics of personal interest
- Express personal opinions with simple reasoning
- Organize text into introduction, body, and conclusion (basic)

## Assessment Criteria for B1 Writing

### Task Achievement
- Addresses the task
- Main ideas are relevant but may be insufficiently developed
- Format may be inappropriately applied

### Coherence and Cohesion
- Information and ideas are organized coherently
- Uses basic cohesive devices (and, but, because, so)
- Paragraphing is used but may be inconsistent

### Lexical Resource
- Uses adequate vocabulary for familiar topics
- Attempts to use less common vocabulary with some success
- Some spelling errors but meaning is clear

### Grammatical Range and Accuracy
- Uses a mix of simple and complex sentence forms
- Frequent grammatical errors but meaning is clear
- Basic tenses are used with reasonable accuracy

## Common B1 Writing Errors

1. **Article misuse**: omitting or overusing a/an/the
2. **Tense inconsistency**: switching between past and present inappropriately
3. **Preposition errors**: in/on/at confusion, especially for time and place
4. **Subject-verb agreement**: third person singular -s omission
5. **Run-on sentences**: joining independent clauses without conjunctions

## Scoring Guide

B1 writing typically scores:
- IELTS: 4.0-5.0
- TOEFL iBT: 13-21 (writing section)
- CEFR: B1
