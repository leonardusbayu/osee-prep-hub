# IELTS Writing Task 2 Assessment Criteria

## Task Requirements

IELTS Writing Task 2 requires candidates to:
- Write at least 250 words
- Respond to a point of view, argument, or problem
- Complete in 40 minutes
- Use formal academic register

## Assessment Criteria (Band 0-9)

### Task Achievement (TA)

**Band 9**: Fully addresses all parts of the task; presents a fully developed position with relevant, extended, and supported ideas.

**Band 7**: Addresses all parts of the task; presents a clear position throughout; presents, extends, and supports main ideas, but there may be a tendency to over-generalize or there may be a lack of supporting examples.

**Band 5**: Addresses the task only partially; format may be inappropriate in places; expresses a position but development is not always clear.

**Band 3**: Does not adequately address any part of the task; does not express a clear position; few relevant ideas.

### Coherence and Cohesion (CC)

**Band 9**: Uses cohesion in such a way that it attracts no attention; skillfully manages paragraphing; uses a range of cohesive devices appropriately.

**Band 7**: Logically organizes information and ideas; clear progression throughout; uses a range of cohesive devices appropriately.

**Band 5**: Presents information with some organization; uses a limited range of cohesive devices; paragraphing may be inadequate.

**Band 3**: Does not organize ideas logically; may use a very limited range of cohesive devices; no paragraphing or unclear.

### Lexical Resource (LR)

**Band 9**: Uses a wide range of vocabulary with very natural and sophisticated control; rare minor errors occur only as slips.

**Band 7**: Uses sufficient vocabulary to allow some flexibility; uses less common vocabulary; may produce occasional errors in word choice and collocation.

**Band 5**: Uses a limited range of vocabulary; attempts to use paraphrase but with mixed success; errors in word choice may cause some difficulty for the reader.

**Band 3**: Uses only a very limited range of words; errors in word choice cause severe difficulty for the reader.

### Grammatical Range and Accuracy (GRA)

**Band 9**: Uses a wide range of structures with full flexibility and accuracy; rare minor errors occur only as slips.

**Band 7**: Uses a variety of complex structures; produces frequent error-free sentences; good control of grammar and punctuation.

**Band 5**: Uses only a limited range of structures; attempts complex sentences but these tend to be less accurate; makes grammatical errors that cause some difficulty.

**Band 3**: Attempts basic sentence forms but with limited success; errors in grammar and punctuation cause severe difficulty.

## Common IELTS Task 2 Topics

1. Education (school vs. university, teaching methods)
2. Technology (impact on society, social media)
3. Environment (climate change, conservation)
4. Health (diet, exercise, healthcare)
5. Society (crime, immigration, urbanization)
6. Work (job satisfaction, remote work)
7. Transport (public vs. private, infrastructure)

## Essay Structure Template

**Introduction (40-50 words)**:
- Paraphrase the topic
- State thesis/position
- Outline main points

**Body Paragraph 1 (80-100 words)**:
- Topic sentence (main idea)
- Explanation
- Example
- Link back to thesis

**Body Paragraph 2 (80-100 words)**:
- Topic sentence (opposing/complementary view)
- Explanation
- Example
- Link back to thesis

**Conclusion (30-40 words)**:
- Restate position
- Summarize main points
- Final thought (optional)
