# CEFR B2 Writing Descriptors

## Overall Written Production

At B2 level, writers can:
- Write clear, detailed texts on a wide range of subjects
- Write an essay or report passing on information or giving reasons in support of/against a particular point of view
- Write letters emphasizing the personal significance of events and experiences

## Writing Formats

### Essays
B2 writers can:
- Develop arguments systematically with supporting evidence
- Use appropriate academic register
- Synthesize information from multiple sources
- Evaluate different viewpoints

### Reports
B2 writers can:
- Structure reports with clear sections (introduction, findings, conclusions)
- Present factual information accurately
- Make recommendations based on evidence

### Reviews
B2 writers can:
- Write reviews of books, films, or events with personal evaluation
- Use descriptive language and appropriate vocabulary
- Balance summary with critique

## Assessment Criteria for B2 Writing

### Task Achievement
- Addresses all parts of the task
- Main ideas are well-developed and relevant
- Format is appropriate to the task

### Coherence and Cohesion
- Information and ideas are logically organized
- Uses a range of cohesive devices effectively
- Paragraphing is clear and supports coherence

### Lexical Resource
- Uses sufficient vocabulary to discuss topics at length
- Uses less common vocabulary with awareness of style and collocation
- Occasional errors in word choice but meaning is clear

### Grammatical Range and Accuracy
- Uses a variety of complex structures
- Grammar is generally accurate
- Errors are occasional and do not impede communication

## Common B2 Writing Errors

1. **Complex clause construction**: relative clauses, conditional structures
2. **Modal verb usage**: appropriate use of should/would/could for hedging
3. **Article nuance**: zero article vs. definite article in abstract concepts
4. **Passive voice**: overuse in academic writing
5. **Punctuation**: comma splices, semicolon misuse

## Scoring Guide

B2 writing typically scores:
- IELTS: 5.5-6.5
- TOEFL iBT: 22-27 (writing section)
- CEFR: B2
